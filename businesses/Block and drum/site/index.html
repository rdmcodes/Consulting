The Concept

Experience the true essence of Atlanta,
distilled into one unique venue.

Born from founder Justin Staples' vision to flip the distillery model — where 85% of the space is built for YOU, not production. Block & Drum is a creative space built for music, spirits, art, and community on Chamblee's Rail Trail.

The name? "Block" is a drummer's woodblock. "Drum" is the barrel-shaped vessel used in distilling. Where rhythm meets craft.

The Farm

Grown here.
Distilled here.

A 40-foot shipping container producing three acres worth of herbs and botanicals using vertical hydroponic technology. Rainwater harvested. Red-blue LED grow lights. Mint, basil, dandelion, honeysuckle — grown steps from where they'll end up in your glass.

Visitors can walk through the farm, learn about urban agriculture, and hand-select the botanicals that go into their custom spirit.
