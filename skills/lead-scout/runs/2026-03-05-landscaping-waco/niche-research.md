### BrightView (brightview.com)

- Image slider with smooth zoom effects in hero

## 2. Must-Have Sections (Page Structure)

### Above the Fold (Hero Area)
1. **Hero Image/Video** — Full-width, high-quality image of a beautifully landscaped yard (lush green lawn, trimmed hedges, colorful flower beds). Ideally a real photo from the business, or a stunning Central Texas landscape. Slight overlay for text readability.
2. **Headline** — Bold, benefit-driven. Examples:
   - "Your Yard, Transformed. Waco's Trusted Lawn Care Experts."
   - "Beautiful Lawns Start Here. Serving Central Texas Since [Year]."
   - "Curb Appeal That Makes the Neighbors Jealous."
3. **Subheadline** — Supporting detail: "Professional mowing, landscaping, and lawn maintenance for homes and businesses across Waco and Central Texas."
4. **Primary CTA Button** — "Get Your Free Estimate" (high-contrast button)
5. **Phone Number** — Click-to-call, visible in header/hero area
6. **Trust Badge Row** — Small icons: "Licensed & Insured | 5-Star Rated | Locally Owned"

### Section 2: Services Grid
- Visual card layout (icon + title + 1-line description) for each service
- 6-8 services displayed with clean icons
- Link to individual service detail pages
- Seasonal highlight capability (swap featured service by season)

### Section 3: Before & After Gallery
- Side-by-side slider (drag to reveal) showing transformations
- 4-6 projects minimum
- Captions with service type and neighborhood/area
- "View Full Gallery" link

### Section 4: Why Choose Us / Trust Signals
- 3-4 key differentiators in icon+text format:
  - "Locally Owned & Operated"
  - "Licensed & Insured"
  - "X+ Years Serving Central Texas"
  - "100% Satisfaction Guaranteed"
- Google review rating badge or embedded reviews widget
- Association logos (if applicable): NALP, local chamber of commerce

### Section 5: Testimonials
- Rotating slider or stacked cards with customer quotes
- First name + last initial + neighborhood (e.g., "Sarah M., Woodway")
- Star rating display
- Photo of the customer or their yard (if available)

### Section 6: About / Meet the Team
- Personal story — who started the business, why they love landscaping
- Team photo or owner photo
- Years in business, family-owned narrative
- "We treat your lawn like our own" messaging

### Section 7: Service Area Map
- Embedded Google Map or styled map graphic
- List of served cities/neighborhoods: Waco, Woodway, Hewitt, Robinson, China Spring, Lorena, McGregor, Bellmead, Lacy Lakeview
- "Not sure if we serve your area? Call us!"

### Section 8: Seasonal Services / Special Offers
- Rotating banner or card highlighting current seasonal service
- Examples: "Spring Cleanup Special" / "Fall Leaf Removal" / "Summer Lawn Fertilization Program"
- Urgency-driven CTA: "Book Before [Date] and Save 10%"

### Section 9: Free Estimate Form (Repeated)
- Simple form: Name, Phone, Email, Address, Service Needed (dropdown), Message
- Keep it short — 5-6 fields max
- "We'll get back to you within 24 hours"
- Should appear at bottom of page AND optionally in hero

### Section 10: Footer
- Business name, address, phone, email
- Business hours
- Service area list
- Quick links: Services, About, Gallery, Contact, Privacy Policy
- Social media icons (Facebook, Instagram, Google)
- "Licensed & Insured" reminder
- Copyright with current year

## 3. Color Palette Recommendation

### Primary Palette — "Central Texas Green"

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary Green | Rich lawn green | `#2D6A4F` | Headers, buttons, nav accents |
| Secondary Green | Fresh grass | `#52B788` | Hover states, highlights, icons |
| Accent Gold/Tan | Warm earth tone | `#D4A373` | CTA buttons, accent borders, highlights |
| Dark Base | Deep charcoal | `#2B2D42` | Body text, footer background |
| Light Background | Warm off-white | `#FAF9F6` | Page background, card backgrounds |
| White | Clean white | `#FFFFFF` | Card surfaces, text on dark |

### Supporting Colors
- **Success/Fresh:** `#95D5B2` (light mint for success states, badges)
- **Warm Brown:** `#8B6F47` (earth tone for borders, subtle accents)
- **Sky Blue:** `#87CEEB` (optional for service area map, irrigation references)
- **Error/Urgency:** `#E63946` (form validation, limited-time offers)

### Color Psychology
- **Green:** Growth, nature, freshness, health — directly maps to landscaping
- **Gold/Tan:** Warmth, earth, reliability, Central Texas soil tones
- **Charcoal:** Professionalism, trust, readability
- **Off-white:** Clean, open, inviting — avoids sterile pure-white

### Gradient Option
- Hero overlay: linear-gradient from `rgba(45, 106, 79, 0.7)` to `rgba(43, 45, 66, 0.6)` over hero image for text readability while maintaining natural feel

## 4. Trust Signals Specific to Landscaping

### Must-Have Trust Elements
1. **"Licensed & Insured"** — Prominently displayed, ideally with a small shield icon
2. **Years in Business** — "Serving Waco Since [Year]" or "X+ Years of Experience"
3. **Photos of Completed Work** — Real projects, not stock photos. Before/after pairs are most powerful.
4. **Customer Testimonials** — With names, neighborhoods, and star ratings
5. **Google Reviews Badge** — Embedded widget or "4.9 Stars on Google (X+ Reviews)"
6. **Locally Owned & Operated** — Central Texas residents trust local businesses
7. **Family-Owned** — If applicable, this resonates strongly in Waco market
8. **BBB Rating / Chamber Membership** — If available
9. **Satisfaction Guarantee** — "100% Satisfaction Guaranteed or We'll Make It Right"
10. **Response Time Promise** — "Free Estimates Within 24-48 Hours"

### Visual Trust Signals
- Branded truck/equipment photos (shows legitimacy and investment)
- Team photos in uniform (professionalism)
- Payment method badges (Visa, MC, etc.)
- "Serving [X] Happy Customers" counter

### Competitor Gap: What Waco Competitors Are Missing
Based on research of local competitors (254 Lawns, Jason's Lawn Care, Ortega's, Fitzgerald's):
- Most lack before/after galleries
- Few have embedded Google reviews on their sites
- Limited use of video content
- Stock photos used instead of real project photos (Jason's has ocean/nature stock images in their gallery)
- Weak or missing seasonal service promotions
- No urgency-driven CTAs or limited-time offers
- **Opportunity:** A well-designed landing page with real imagery, strong social proof, and clear seasonal CTAs will stand out significantly in this market

## 5. CTA Strategy

### Primary CTA: Free Estimate / Free Quote
- **Button Text Options:**
  - "Get Your Free Estimate"
  - "Request a Free Quote"
  - "Get a Personalized Lawn Care Quote"
- **Placement:** Hero section, sticky header, mid-page, bottom of page
- **Style:** High-contrast button (gold/tan `#D4A373` on green, or bright green on white)
- **Supporting text:** "No obligation. We'll respond within 24 hours."

### Secondary CTA: Call Now
- **Button Text:** "Call (254) XXX-XXXX" with phone icon
- **Placement:** Header (always visible), hero section, footer
- **Mobile:** Click-to-call button, sticky bottom bar on mobile
- **Style:** Outlined button or text link alongside primary CTA

### Tertiary CTAs (Supporting)
- "View Our Work" — links to gallery/portfolio
- "See Our Services" — links to services section
- "Read Reviews" — links to testimonials

### CTA Rules
- Every scroll-depth should have a visible CTA within one screen height
- Primary CTA uses the strongest contrast color in the palette
- Never more than 2 CTAs competing in the same visual area
- Mobile: sticky bottom bar with "Call" and "Free Quote" buttons
- Forms should have minimal fields (Name, Phone, Service = minimum viable)

## 6. Content Tone & Voice

### Overall Tone: Professional but Friendly
- Confident without being arrogant
- Warm and approachable — like talking to a knowledgeable neighbor
- Results-focused — emphasize transformation and outcomes
- Locally rooted — reference Waco, Central Texas, specific neighborhoods

### Key Messaging Themes
1. **Transformation** — "From overgrown to outstanding"
2. **Curb Appeal** — "The yard that makes your neighbors jealous"
3. **Reliability** — "Show up on time, every time"
4. **Local Pride** — "Your neighbors trust us. You will too."
5. **Peace of Mind** — "Sit back and enjoy your weekend — we've got the lawn covered"
6. **Seasonal Relevance** — Reference Central Texas weather patterns and seasonal needs

### Headline Examples
- "Beautiful Lawns, Happy Homeowners. That's Our Thing."
- "Waco's Go-To Lawn Care Team Since [Year]."
- "Your Lawn Deserves Better. Let's Make It Happen."
- "From First Mow to Full Makeover — We Do It All."
- "Central Texas Heat? We've Got Your Lawn Covered."

### Words to USE
- Transform, refresh, revitalize, maintain, enhance, beautify
- Curb appeal, lush, green, healthy, manicured, pristine
- Professional, reliable, trusted, local, experienced
- Free estimate, no obligation, satisfaction guaranteed

### Words to AVOID
- Cheap, discount (use "affordable" or "competitive" instead)
- Technical jargon (soil pH, cultivar names — keep it simple)
- Negative framing ("Don't let your yard..." — reframe positively)
- "We're the best" (let testimonials say it for you)

## 8. Central Texas / Waco Market Notes

### Climate & Seasonal Considerations
- **USDA Zone 8a/8b** — Hot summers (100F+), mild winters, occasional freeze
- **Primary Grass Types:** Bermuda grass (most common), St. Augustine, Zoysia, Buffalo grass
- **Growing Season:** March through November (long season = more recurring revenue)
- **Key Pests:** Fire ants, grubs, chinch bugs, armyworms
- **Water Restrictions:** Waco periodically enforces watering schedules; drought-tolerant landscaping is a selling point
- **Clay Soil:** Heavy clay soils common in Central TX — aeration services are in high demand

### Local Competition Analysis (Waco)

| Competitor | Website | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|---------|-----------|------------|
| **254 Lawns** | 254lawns.com | Local owner story, service gallery, free quote form | Dated design, generic imagery, cluttered layout |
| **Jason's Lawn Care** | jasonslawncarewaco.com | 35+ years experience, hero form, new customer discount | Stock images in gallery (ocean/forest!), template feel, broken placeholder text |
| **Ortega's Lawn & Landscaping** | ortegaslawn.com | Tree service focus, free estimates, honest messaging | Very basic template design, minimal gallery, limited trust signals |
| **Fitzgerald's** | fitzltd.com | Full-service (landscape + irrigation + tree care), certified arborist, professional branding | Better than competitors but still template-based, could improve CTA placement |

### Key Opportunity
The Waco landscaping market has **no standout websites**. Most competitors use template-based designs with stock photos, limited social proof, and weak CTAs. A modern, visually stunning landing page with real imagery, strong conversion elements, and clear seasonal messaging will significantly outperform the local competition.

## 9. Design & UX Requirements

### Layout
- Single-page landing page with smooth scroll sections
- Sticky header with logo, phone number, and primary CTA button
- Mobile-first responsive design (most homeowners search on mobile)
- Page load under 3 seconds

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat, or Poppins) — clean and modern
- **Body:** Readable sans-serif at 16px+ (same family or complementary)
- **Accent:** Optional serif for testimonial quotes (adds warmth and personality)

### Imagery Requirements
- High-resolution photos of lush green lawns, manicured hedges, colorful flower beds
- Central Texas-appropriate plants and landscapes (not Pacific Northwest or tropical)
- Before/after transformation photos (most impactful visual element)
- Team/truck photos for credibility
- Avoid generic stock photos — they kill trust

### Mobile-Specific
- Sticky bottom bar: "Call Now" + "Free Estimate" side by side
- Hamburger menu with prominent phone number
- Touch-friendly form fields and buttons (44px+ tap targets)
- Simplified gallery (swipeable carousel)

### Performance
- Optimize images (WebP format, lazy loading)
- Minimal JavaScript — fast initial load
- Core Web Vitals compliant (LCP < 2.5s, CLS < 0.1)

## 10. Summary: What Will Win in This Market

1. **Real photos over stock** — Immediately sets apart from every Waco competitor
2. **Hero-level lead capture** — Form or CTA above the fold, not buried at bottom
3. **Before/after gallery** — The single most persuasive visual element for landscaping
4. **Local, personal story** — "We're your Waco neighbors" resonates in this market
5. **Seasonal relevance** — Rotate hero content and CTAs by season (Central TX has distinct needs)
6. **Mobile-first design** — Clean, fast, thumb-friendly — homeowners search on their phones
7. **Social proof everywhere** — Google reviews, testimonials, years in business, satisfaction guarantee
8. **Strong, repeated CTAs** — "Free Estimate" at every scroll depth
9. **Earth-tone color palette** — Greens and tans that feel natural and trustworthy
10. **Speed and simplicity** — Don't overcomplicate it. Clear message, clear action, beautiful work.
