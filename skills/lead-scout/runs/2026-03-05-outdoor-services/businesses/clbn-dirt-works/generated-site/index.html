What We Do

Our Services

From clearing raw land to building rock-solid driveways, we handle the tough stuff so your property looks and works the way it should.

Gravel Driveways

New builds, repairs, and regrading. We use quality materials that hold up to Texas weather and heavy traffic.

Culvert Installation

Proper drainage culverts sized and installed right the first time. Keep water flowing where it should.

Fence Line Clearing

Clear brush and debris along fence lines for new fencing or to maintain existing property boundaries.

Brush Hogging

Reclaim overgrown fields and pastures. Heavy-duty mowing for thick brush, tall grass, and rough terrain.

Land Clearing

Full lot and acreage clearing for new construction, farming, or property improvement. Trees, stumps, and all.

Grading & Leveling

Precision grading for proper drainage, building pads, and smooth surfaces. We get the slope right.

Drainage Solutions

Fix standing water and erosion problems with proper grading, ditching, and drainage systems.

General Dirt Work

Hauling, fill dirt, top soil, excavation, and whatever else your property needs. If it involves dirt, we do it.

About Us

Built on Hard Work & Handshakes

CLBN Dirt Works is a locally owned and operated gravel and land services business right here in Corsicana, Texas. We got into this business because we saw too many property owners getting overcharged for subpar work.

We're committed to providing reliable service, quality materials, and honest pricing to every customer, whether it's a small driveway repair or a full land clearing project. We treat every property like it's our own.

When you hire CLBN, you're hiring your neighbors. We live here, we work here, and our reputation is everything. That means we do the job right the first time and stand behind our work.
