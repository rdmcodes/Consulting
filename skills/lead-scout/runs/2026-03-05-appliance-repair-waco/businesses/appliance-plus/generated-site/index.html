Meet Brian & Jill

Appliance Plus isn't just a business—it's a family mission. Brian and Jill C. started Appliance Plus with a simple belief: when something breaks in your home, you deserve someone who shows up on time, tells you the truth about what's wrong, and charges a fair price to fix it.

Based right here in Hewitt, TX, they've built their reputation one repair at a time. Whether it's a fridge on the fritz or a washing machine flooding your laundry room, Brian brings the technical know-how and Jill keeps everything running smoothly behind the scenes.

When you call Appliance Plus, you're not getting a faceless corporation. You're getting a family that takes pride in every single job.
