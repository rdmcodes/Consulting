## 2. Must-Have Sections (Top to Bottom)

### A. Sticky Header / Top Bar
- Phone number (click-to-call on mobile)
- "Call Now" button (primary CTA)
- Business hours badge (e.g., "Open Today 8am-6pm")
- Optional: "Book Online" secondary button

### B. Hero Section
- **Headline:** Bold, benefit-driven (e.g., "Same-Day Appliance Repair in Waco, TX")
- **Subheadline:** Supporting value prop (e.g., "Licensed technicians. Upfront pricing. All brands serviced.")
- **Hero image/video:** Technician working on an appliance in a real kitchen, or clean modern kitchen with appliances
- **Primary CTA:** Large "Call Now" or "Schedule Service" button
- **Trust badges row:** "Same Day Service" | "Licensed & Insured" | "All Brands" | "Satisfaction Guaranteed"

### C. Services / Appliance Types Grid
- Icon cards for each appliance type (see Section 6 for full list)
- Each card: icon + appliance name + brief one-liner
- Links to individual service detail (if multi-page) or scrolls to description

### D. Why Choose Us / Value Props
- 3-4 columns or cards highlighting differentiators:
  - "Same Day / Next Day Service"
  - "Upfront, Honest Pricing — No Hidden Fees"
  - "Factory Certified Technicians"
  - "1-Year Parts & Labor Warranty"

### E. How It Works (Process Steps)
- Step 1: "Call or Book Online"
- Step 2: "We Diagnose the Problem"
- Step 3: "Fast, Reliable Repair"
- Simple 3-step visual with icons or numbered badges

### F. Quote / Service Request Form
- Fields: Name, Phone, Email, Appliance Type (dropdown), Brief Description
- Prominent placement — can be a floating sidebar on desktop or inline section
- "Get Your Free Quote" or "Request Service" button

### G. Customer Reviews / Testimonials
- 3-5 real reviews with star ratings
- Google Reviews badge or rating widget
- Customer name and city for local credibility

### H. Brands We Service
- Logo strip of major appliance brands
- Key brands: Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, Maytag, KitchenAid, Frigidaire, Bosch, Kenmore, Amana, Electrolux, Sub-Zero, Viking, Thermador

### I. Service Area
- "Serving Waco & Central Texas" with list of cities:
  - Waco, Woodway, Hewitt, Lorena, Robinson, Bellmead, Lacy Lakeview, China Spring, McGregor, Temple, Killeen, Belton, Harker Heights
- Optional: embedded Google Map with service radius

### J. About / Meet the Team (Brief)
- 2-3 sentences about the business
- Photo of owner or team (personal touch is a differentiator vs. faceless competitors)
- Years in business, family-owned emphasis if applicable

### K. FAQ Section
- 5-8 common questions:
  - "How quickly can you come out?"
  - "Do you charge a service/diagnostic fee?"
  - "What brands do you repair?"
  - "Do you offer a warranty on repairs?"
  - "How much does appliance repair cost?"
  - "Should I repair or replace my appliance?"
  - "Do you carry parts with you?"

### L. Footer
- Phone number (repeated)
- Business hours
- Service area summary
- License/insurance info
- Links: Privacy Policy, Terms
- Social media icons (Google, Facebook, Yelp)

## 5. CTA Strategy

### Primary CTA: "Call Now"
- Click-to-call phone number
- Appears in: sticky header, hero section, floating mobile button
- Color: Bright orange or green on contrasting background
- Format: `(254) XXX-XXXX` — always visible
- Mobile: Sticky bottom bar with "Tap to Call" button

### Secondary CTA: "Request Service" / "Get a Free Quote"
- Leads to inline form or modal
- Appears in: hero section, mid-page, near testimonials
- Fields: Name, Phone, Email, Appliance Type, Problem Description
- Submit button text: "Get My Free Quote" or "Schedule Service"

### Tertiary CTAs (supporting)
- "Text Us" — for SMS-preferred customers
- "Book Online" — for self-service scheduling
- Live chat widget (lower right corner)

### CTA Placement Rules
- Every scroll-depth of ~600px should have a CTA visible
- Mobile: persistent bottom bar with phone + form buttons
- After testimonials section: "Ready to get your appliance fixed? Call now!"
- After FAQ: "Still have questions? We're here to help — call or text"

## 7. Content Tone & Messaging Guidelines

### Voice
- **Professional but approachable** — not corporate, not too casual
- **Confident and reassuring** — "We've got this handled"
- **Empathetic** — acknowledge the frustration of a broken appliance
- **Local** — mention Waco/Central Texas naturally

### Key Messaging Themes
1. **Speed** — "Same day service" is the #1 message in this niche
2. **Trust** — Licensed, insured, warranty, upfront pricing
3. **Expertise** — All brands, factory certified, experienced technicians
4. **Convenience** — Easy booking, flexible scheduling, we come to you
5. **Value** — Fair pricing, repair vs. replace savings, no hidden fees

### Sample Headlines
- "Fast, Reliable Appliance Repair in Waco, TX"
- "Your Appliances Fixed Today — Guaranteed"
- "Waco's Most Trusted Appliance Repair Service"
- "Broken Appliance? We'll Fix It Same Day."
- "Expert Repair for Every Appliance in Your Home"

### Sample Subheadlines
- "Licensed technicians servicing all major brands with upfront pricing"
- "From refrigerators to dryers — we fix it all, fast"
- "Serving Waco, Woodway, Hewitt & all of Central Texas"

### Words to Use
- Same day, fast, reliable, trusted, expert, certified, licensed, insured, affordable, upfront, honest, guaranteed, local, professional, experienced

### Words to Avoid
- Cheap (use "affordable"), complicated, eventually, maybe, try

## 8. Mobile-First Design Notes

- 60%+ of appliance repair searches happen on mobile (urgent need)
- Sticky call button at bottom of screen is critical
- Form fields must be thumb-friendly (large tap targets)
- Hero section must load fast — avoid heavy video on mobile
- Service icons should be in a 2-column grid on mobile
- Reviews should be in a horizontal swipe carousel
- Reduce text density — use bullet points over paragraphs
