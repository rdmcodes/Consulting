# Digital Consulting — Local Business Growth Agency

A two-person consulting operation that helps local businesses improve their online presence. We find businesses with weak or no web presence, build them a free demo site to prove value, and convert them to paying clients across a range of digital services.

---

## What We Do

- **Website Development & Maintenance** — modern, mobile-first, conversion-optimized sites
- **SEO** — Google Business Profile optimization, local keyword strategy
- **Social Media** — content creation, visuals, account management
- **Workflow Automation** — booking systems, email/SMS marketing, CRM setup
- **Monthly Audits** — 30-day report of everything done, delivered in-person or via Zoom

---

## How It Works

```
Find Leads → Research & Audit → Build Demo Site → Deploy → Outreach → Sales Call → Ongoing Services
```

1. Scrape Google Maps, Yelp, and Facebook for businesses in a target niche + city
2. Audit each business's web presence (site, Google profile, social media, reviews)
3. Build a polished demo site using their real brand colors, photos, and content
4. Deploy it live and reach out casually with the link
5. Convert to a paying client and grow the relationship over time
